Speech based music player in Android Application and Python

Project Description: Developed a speech-based music player application for Android and Python platforms. The application allows users to play music and provides personalized music recommendations based on natural language processing (NLP) integration and machine learning (ML) algorithms. Users can use voice commands to control the music playback and interact with the application. The application is integrated with online music platforms to fetch and recommend relevant music based on the user's preferences and listening habits. It provides an intuitive and interactive music experience for users, making it easy and enjoyable to discover and listen to their favorite songs.
