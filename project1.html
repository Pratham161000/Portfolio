Website Development for Customer Relationship Management (CRM)

Project Description: Developed a web-based CRM application for the ICT department of Marwadi University. The application is designed to streamline and manage the entire process from inquiry to enrollment and beyond. It provides efficient management of applicant and inquiry data, facilitating effective communication with prospective students. The CRM application also offers analytics and reporting features, allowing the university to gain insights and make data-driven decisions. With its user-friendly interface and comprehensive functionalities, the CRM application enhances the efficiency and effectiveness of managing student relationships and contributes to the overall success of the university's operations.
